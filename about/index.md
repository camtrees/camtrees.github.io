# {{ page.title }}

This website was created by [Kenster Rosenberry](mailto:ken.rosenberry@gmail.com).
Illustrations were conceptualized by Kenster then further refined and brought to
life by [ChatGPT's Image Generator](https://chatgpt.com/images/).

My experience using ChatGPT to create the illustrations used on this website blew me away!
I found the experience quite similar to working with a real human designer/artist. What
most impressed me was the reasoning ChatGPT gave for why it thought the images worked so
well and what my, and more often its, design goals were for the illustrations.

Check out this slightly edited transcript of
[my ChatGPT dialogue](../assets/images/website/chatgpt-website-illustrations.pdf)
to see if you will be as equally impressed and amazed as I was.
